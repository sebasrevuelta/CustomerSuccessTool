How Health Score Is Calculated

Health Score is the rounded average of the enabled factor scores: Last Activity (days_since_last_contact_score), Contributors (contributors_score), Health AE (health_ae_score), and Feature Request (critical_fr_score). Enabling a factor includes it in the average; disabling a factor removes it from the calculation. This can increase or decrease the final Health Score depending on that factor's value for each account.
